# Solar Industry Domain Knowledge

## LEAD SCORING

| Criteria | Points | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Homeowner | +20 | Renters cannot install |
| Bill > $150/month | +15 | Higher bill = more savings |
| Good roof condition | +15 | <15 years old, no major damage |
| Timeline < 6 months | +20 | Ready to act |
| No HOA restrictions | +10 | HOA can block installation |
| South-facing roof | +10 | Best sun exposure |

**Qualified Lead: 50+ points**

## QUALIFICATION QUESTIONS

Must collect (in order):
1. Property type - Single family, multi-family, commercial
2. Ownership - Own or rent
3. Roof condition - Age, material, shading issues
4. Monthly electric bill - Average amount
5. Location - Zip code for sun exposure
6. Timeline - When looking to install

## INCENTIVES & BENEFITS

- **30% Federal Tax Credit (ITC)** - Available through 2032
- **State/local incentives** - Vary by location
- **Net metering** - Credits for excess energy
- **Energy independence** - Reduced grid dependency
- **Property value** - 4% average increase
- **Environmental impact** - ~3-4 tons CO2 offset/year

## COMMON OBJECTIONS

| Objection | Response Strategy |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Too expensive | $0 down options, tax credits, monthly savings offset |
| Roof too old | Can bundle roof replacement with install |
| Moving soon | Increases home value, transfers to buyer |
| Not enough sun | Modern panels efficient in various conditions |
| HOA restrictions | Many states have solar access laws |

## DISQUALIFICATION HANDLING

When lead doesn't qualify (renter, very low bill, bad roof):
1. Acknowledge interest
2. Explain why solar may not fit right now
3. Suggest alternatives:
   - Community solar programs
   - Energy efficiency improvements
   - Check back when conditions change
4. Remain helpful and positive

## SYSTEM SIZING GUIDELINES

| Monthly Bill | Estimated System | Monthly Savings |
|--------------|------------------|-----------------|
| $100-150 | 5-6 kW | $60-90 |
| $150-250 | 7-9 kW | $100-160 |
| $250-400 | 10-14 kW | $170-280 |
| $400+ | 15+ kW | $280+ |

Payback period: Typically 6-10 years depending on location and incentives.
