    Knowledge Base

    About Peritus Learning

    Peritus Learning is a UK-based e-learning company revolutionizing corporate training through unlimited-user pricing.

    Target Audience

    The newsletter targets:

    • L&D Managers
    • HR Professionals
    • Finance Directors

    Newsletter Format

    The newsletter follows a B2B email marketing format with professional tone and structure:

    • Hero Article: Main feature (300-400 words)
    • Quick Insights: Three shorter sections (100-150 words each)
    • Call to Action: Closing section (50-75 words)

    Content Pillars

    Each newsletter section is built around one of these five content pillars:

    1. Cost Savings & ROI Spotlight: Focuses on financial benefits and return on investment
    2. L&D Quick Wins & Best Practices: Practical tips and strategies for learning and development
    3. Product & Feature Spotlight: Highlights Peritus Learning's offerings and capabilities
    4. Industry Insights & Thought Leadership: Trends, research, and forward-thinking perspectives
    5. Events & Community: Information about upcoming events, webinars, and community engagement
